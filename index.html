The Journey (so far)

September 2022, I was just a curious web surfer, fascinated by how websites worked. Then I discovered front-end development—HTML, CSS, JavaScript—and got hooked. By October 2023, I built GuessMaster, a simple number-guessing game that I leveled up with multi-level logic. Not groundbreaking, but it proved I could build something real. A month later, I graduated as an economist, though being a programmer had already stolen the spotlight. Mid-2024, I got serious—learning Python, web scraping, automation, and Django. I built a Google Maps scraper, got into GitHub, and dove into full-stack development. March 2025 was a win— I earned my first tech certification in JavaScript DSA from freeCodeCamp. The sky? It's starting to look like the beginning. The ride's been wild, and I'm just getting started.
